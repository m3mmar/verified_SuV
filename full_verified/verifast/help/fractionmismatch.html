VeriFast Help > Error messages

Error message

Fraction mismatch: cannot prove f == g or 0 < f < g

Context

When consuming a field assertion or predicate assertion.

Cause

The symbolic heap contains a matching chunk, but the fraction required by the assertion is larger than the fraction found in the matching heap chunk.

Notes

Suggested solutions

Lower the fraction required by the assertion or increase the fraction of the chunk in the symbolic heap. Suggested approaches:

  • Lower the fraction required by the assertion, i.e. replace the assertion's current fraction by a smaller expression, an existential variable or a dummy fraction.
  • If the code preceding the assertion leaks the heap chunk, remove the leak command.
  • Use one of the solutions of No matching heap chunks.
