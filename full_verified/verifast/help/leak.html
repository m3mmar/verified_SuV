Error message

Function/Loop leaks heap chunks

Context

After consuming the postcondition of a function or the invariant of a loop.

Cause

The symbolic heap is not empty at the end of a function or loop.

Notes

  • Heap chunks with a dummy fraction are ignored by the leak check.

Suggested solutions

Ensure that the symbolic heap is empty. Suggested approaches:

  • Add additional conjuncts to the postcondition or loop invariant such that the remaining chunks are consumed.
  • If the remaining chunk is predicate assertion which represents an empty heap, open the predicate.
  • Leak the remaining chunk using the leak command.
