Term

Symbolic heap

Definition

The set of heap chunks currently owned by the current function.

Shown in the Heap chunks pane in the bottom right corner of the VeriFast window.

Notes

  • To show the symbolic heap at a given symbolic execution step, select the step in the Steps pane.
  • Producing a field assertion or predicate assertion adds a matching chunk to the symbolic heap.
  • Consuming a field assertion or predicate assertion removes a matching chunk from the symbolic heap.
  • The path condition, the symbolic store, and the symbolic heap together constitute the symbolic state of the symbolic execution.
